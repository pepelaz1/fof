Swivels are an essential piece of equipment in making up rigs. When   joining   main   lines   to   traces   or   weights   to   booms   the   swivel will   help   stop   tangles   and   assist   in   the   recovery   of   species   that have the tendency to spin as you retrieve. Swivels   come   in   different   sizes   and   styles,   as   shown   below,   but   all have   the   same   function   to   join   two   lines   together   (as   well   as   the ones to fix the weight).
